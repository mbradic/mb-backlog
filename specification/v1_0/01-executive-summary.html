1. Executive Summary

mb-backlog is a personal application designed for managing and maintaining a personal backlog of tasks, projects, and ideas. The application provides users with a centralized platform to organize, prioritize, and track their personal work items. This requirements specification outlines the functional and non-functional requirements for version 1.0 of the mb-backlog application.

Key Objectives:

  • Provide a centralized platform for personal backlog management
  • Enable easy creation, organization, and prioritization of tasks
  • Facilitate task tracking and progress monitoring
  • Support flexible categorization and filtering of work items
  • Ensure intuitive user experience for personal productivity
